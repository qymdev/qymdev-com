/01

How it works

An air-pressure sensor, your height, and a haptic at the final rakat.

PrayerCount keeps track of your rakats using the watch's built-in air-pressure sensor. When you bow into sujood the air pressure rises; when you stand again, it falls. That difference is enough to advance the count.

Set your height during setup and it should detect your rakats. If that doesn't work for your specific prayer form, run the optional guided calibration that captures your standing-to-sujood drop so detection fits the way you pray.

A gentle haptic at the final rakat lets you finish without looking at the screen. Choice of haptic-only or haptic-with-sound for the completion cue.

Selection & timing

/03.B

Madhab

Shafi/Maliki/Hanbali or Hanafi for Asr timing. Switchable in Settings.

Manual prayer selection

Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha, Witr.

/03.D

Custom prayer selection

2- or 4-rakat configurations for nafl and sunnah.

/03.E

Optional sunnah

Turn it on in Settings to walk through sunnah before → fard → sunnah after at each prayer. Skip a step and the suggestion moves to the next one. Picks up where you left off if you close the app. Starts fresh each day.

/03.F

Sunnah schedules

Four presets: Mu’akkadah only (12 rakat), with Asr sunnah (16 rakat), Hanafi maximum (20 rakat), or Match madhab.
