PrayerCount

An Apple Watch rakat counter that just works on your wrist.

PrayerCount keeps track of your rakats during salah using the watch's built-in barometric altimeter. A gentle haptic at the final rakat lets you finish without looking at the screen. Runs entirely on the watch — no iPhone needed during prayer, no account, no servers, no analytics.

Privacy Policy Contact & Feedback

Features

Counting & detection

  • Automatic rakat counting via the watch's barometric altimeter — no taps during prayer.
  • Guided calibration captures your standing-to-sujood height so detection fits your prayer form.
  • Gentle haptic at the final rakat — finish without glancing at the screen.
  • Choice of haptic-only or haptic-with-sound completion feedback.

Prayer selection & timing

  • Auto-suggests the current prayer based on your location.
  • 12 calculation methods: Muslim World League, ISNA (North America), Egyptian, Karachi, Umm al-Qura (Makkah), Dubai, Moonsighting Committee, Kuwait, Qatar, Singapore, Tehran, and Diyanet (Turkey).
  • Shafi or Hanafi school of thought for Asr timing.
  • Manual selection for Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha, and Witr.
  • Custom 2-rakat and 4-rakat sessions for nafl and sunnah prayers.

Optional sunnah prayers

  • Opt-in toggle that walks you through the sunnah prayers around each daily prayer — the suggestion advances sunnah before → fard → sunnah after automatically.
  • Four schedules to match how you pray: Mu’akkadah only (12 rakat), with Asr sunnah (16 rakat), Hanafi maximum (20 rakat), or Match madhab.
  • Skip-forward: tap Skip on any step (or start the fard directly) and the sequence jumps to the next remaining step in the bracket.
  • Resumes where you left off after a force-quit; resets at day rollover.

Qibla

  • Live Qibla compass with bearing to Mecca, calculated on-device from your location.

History

  • The last 365 days of prayer sessions kept on-device.
  • Daily, weekly, and monthly views plus a chronological list.
  • Export your history as JSON via the system share sheet.

Notifications & integrations

  • Optional on-watch notifications at each daily prayer time.
  • Optional Apple Health workout entry — start time, end time, and prayer name only; toggle off any time.
  • "Start Prayer" Siri shortcut for a one-sentence quick start.
  • Always-On display supported.

Accessibility

  • Full VoiceOver labels and hints throughout.
  • Dynamic Type for all text.
  • Reduce Motion respected during prayer.
  • Double Tap gesture support on Apple Watch Series 9 and later.

Languages

Available in 18 languages: English, العربية (Arabic), বাংলা (Bengali), 简体中文 (Simplified Chinese), 繁體中文 (Traditional Chinese), Français, Deutsch, Hausa, Bahasa Indonesia, 日本語, 한국어, Bahasa Melayu, فارسی (Persian), Русский, Español, Kiswahili, Türkçe, and اردو (Urdu). Right-to-left layout for Arabic, Persian, and Urdu.

Privacy

Designed to keep your prayer data on your watch. No accounts, no servers, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking. Location is rounded to ~1 km before being saved on-device, used only for prayer-time and Qibla calculation, and never transmitted. See the full Privacy Policy.
