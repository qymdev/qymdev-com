/01

How it works

A barometric sensor, a calibration step, and a haptic at the final rakat.

PrayerCount keeps track of your rakats using the watch's built-in altimeter. When you bow into sujood the air pressure rises a touch; when you stand again, it falls. That difference is enough to advance the count. A guided calibration up front captures your standing-to-sujood drop so detection fits the way you pray.

A gentle haptic at the final rakat lets you finish without looking at the screen. Choice of haptic-only or haptic-with-sound for the completion cue.

Custom sessions

2- or 4-rakat configurations for nafl and sunnah.

/03.E

Optional sunnah

Opt-in toggle. Suggestion walks sunnah before → fard → sunnah after automatically. Skip-forward semantics; resumes after force-quit; resets at day rollover.

Sunnah schedules

Four presets: Mu’akkadah only (12 rakat), with Asr sunnah (16 rakat), Hanafi maximum (20 rakat), or Match madhab.
